Welcome our website.

Science52101 is a beginning group of experimental projects that aim for testing and studying technology, computers and general science with software and hardware tools.
It also looks for alternative ways of making software, algorithms and mathematical processes.

–•– Main Aims –•–

A&DSX
Algorithm and Data Structure eXperiments
 There are many possible algorithms to be studied, made and applyed.
We look for pure and professional uses of programming concepts.

TM&EX
Text Manipulation and Evaluation eXperiments
 Text may contain many patterns and standards to be observed and used.
Testing text manipulation and evaluation with linguistic concepts or syntax standards has always been an interest of this project.

CPU&AIX
CPU and AI eXperiments
 Other Science52101 technological experiments are coming.
Most of these are related to CPU and AI, by exploring Assembly and machine learning.

Mathematics
Science52101 is also building new projects related to Mathematics.
We are looking for any possibility of inovation, testing mathematical concepts.

UI&XX
User Interface and eXperience eXperiments
 Since Akai Keisanki, the founder of Science52101 began designing interfaces for fun, this project also aims to develop a better user experience.
Future fixes and studies shall make the experiments and projects more elegant and robust.
